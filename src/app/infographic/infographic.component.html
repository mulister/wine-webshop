🍇🌿🍷Welcome to Georgia’s Wine Legacy 🍇🌿🍷 Georgia, the birthplace of wine, boasts an 8,000-year winemaking heritage. The ancient qvevri method, using clay vessels buried underground, infuses Georgian wines with a unique character and a connection to their rich cultural history.

🍇 A World of Unique Grape Varieties 🎶 Georgia boasts over 500 indigenous grape varieties, each offering a distinctive taste. From the bold Saperavi to the elegant Rkatsiteli, these wines will take your palate on a Georgian adventure.
